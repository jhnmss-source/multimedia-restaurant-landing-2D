Our History

What began in 2026 as a series of "renegade" forest dinners has evolved into a sanctuary for the senses. Founded by foragers Moses and Kristianne, Verdant Ember was born from a desire to return to the primitive roots of cooking.

We stand out because we have abolished the modern stove. Every dish is touched by the spirit of the woods cooked over reclaimed oak, cherrywood, and white-hot river stones.

Our Signature Philosophy

"The flame doesn't just cook the food; it seasons the soul."
